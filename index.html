The man who made all Tunisians smile

Here's a timeline of Sofiane Chaari's Life

  • 1962 - born in Tunis, Tunisia
  • 2001 - "Manamit Arousia".
  • 2002 - "Malla Ana".
  • 2003 - "And Azaiz" as Sadok.
  • 2004 - "Hotel" as Stoukou.
  • 2005 - 2009 - "Choufli hal" as Sbou3i, this is the most famous role in his career with total of 5 seasons, Sofiane became the most loved and respected actor in Tunisia with thje smile he brought to anyone who watched him.
  • 2009 - "Sofiane Show" this where he was a television animator, his show was about kids' talent, earned a huge succes back then in Tunisia as the only show who really cared about children.
  • 2010 - "Nsibtti la3ziza" as Hsouna, this was the last the Tunisian people saw him in, "Nsibti la3ziza" was also the idea of Sofiane and he was an actor in it. But despite this Sofiane suffred a lot in this show because of the production team which made him think of quitting the show.
  • 2011 - died in Marsa,Tunisia of heart attack. Rumors says that he got depressed after the suffer of "Nsibti la3ziza" which was the main reason of the heart attack.
